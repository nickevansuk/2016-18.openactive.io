Online open data course for physical activity

The Open Data Institute are providing a free, online open data e-learning course, including a set of practical modules covering what open data is, its business benefits, how it can be published and prioritised to gain real value from it. Give it a try and send us your feedback!

1. Open data in the physical activity sector

2. Creating impact from open data

3. Prioritising data to publish openly

4. How to make your data useful

Upcoming courses

The Open Data Institute are providing ODI certified training to sport and physical activity organisations to help them understand what open data is and how opening up their data can benefit them, amongst other things.

For more information, and to register for your free place, please click on one of the sessions below. If no sessions are available, please get in touch at hello@openactive.io to register your interest.

RESOURCES TO explain opportunity data

View RESOURCES

The OpenActive message can be freely and easily shared by anyone. These resources are designed to help those who see the benefits of open data in our sector spread the word and move our sector forward. The resources are continually evolving, and comments and contributions are welcome.

Guides and materials

The Open Data Institute has produced several guides to open data. If you are new to opening up your data then the following resources might be of use to you:

  • What makes data open?
  • How will open data affect me?
  • Publisher's Guide to Open Data Licensing
  • What are the impacts of non-open licences?
  • Case studies
  • Engaging with data reusers
About OpenActive

We are a group of organisations who believe that open data will help people get active, coordinated by the Open Data Institute.
